COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING PUNE (COEP)

The College of Engineering Pune (COEP) is a premier engineering institution in Pune, Maharashtra. Established in 1854, COEP has a rich history and has been instrumental in shaping the careers of many distinguished engineers and technologists. The college offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines. COEP is renowned for its rigorous academic curriculum, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and emphasis on research and innovation. The campus is equipped with modern laboratories, libraries, and other facilities that provide an excellent learning environment for students. COEP's commitment to academic excellence and its focus on holistic development make it one of the top engineering colleges in India.
