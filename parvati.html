Parvati Hill

ABOUT PARVATI HILL

Parvati Hill, located in Pune, is a historic site offering panoramic views of the city. The hill is home to the Parvati Temple, which dates back to the 18th century and is dedicated to various deities including Lord Shiva and Goddess Parvati. The hill's serene environment and elevated position make it a popular spot for both tourists and locals. The temple complex consists of several small temples and is surrounded by lush greenery, adding to its tranquil atmosphere. The climb to the hilltop is a rewarding experience, providing a stunning view of Pune's landscape. Parvati Hill is an important landmark in Pune, known for its religious significance and natural beauty.
