Detailed Information About Pune Colleges:

Pune is home to numerous prestigious colleges offering a wide range of programs. Here is more information about some of the prominent colleges and their branches:

1. MIT WORLD PEACE UNIVERSITY

MIT WPU offers programs in engineering, management, law, and humanities. The university is known for its emphasis on research and practical learning.

  • Engineering: Mechanical, Civil, Computer Science
  • Management: MBA, Executive MBA
  • Law: LLB, LLM
  • Humanities: Psychology, Sociology

2. MMCOE

MMCOE provides high-quality education in engineering with a focus on innovation and technical skills.

  • Engineering: Electrical, Electronics, Chemical
  • Management: MBA in Operations, Marketing

3. GARWARE COLLEGE

Garware College specializes in arts, science, and commerce programs. The college is known for its robust academic curriculum and extracurricular activities.

  • Arts: English, History, Philosophy
  • Science: Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • Commerce: B.Com, M.Com

4. FERGUSSON COLLEGE

Fergusson College offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in arts, science, and commerce.

  • Science: Mathematics, Botany, Zoology
  • Arts: Economics, Political Science
  • Commerce: Business Administration

5. COEP

COEP is one of the oldest engineering colleges in India, offering a variety of engineering programs.

  • Engineering: Computer Engineering, Information Technology, Structural Engineering

6. SYMBIOSIS COLLEGEG

Symbiosis College is known for its programs in management, law, and liberal arts.

  • Management: BBA, MBA
  • Law: Integrated LLB
  • Liberal Arts: BA in Journalism, Media Studies
