Pataleshwar Cave Temple

ABOUT PATALESHWAR CAVE TEMPLE

Pataleshwar Cave Temple, situated in Pune, is a historic rock-cut temple dedicated to Lord Shiva. The temple, believed to date back to the 8th century, is carved out of basalt rock and features intricate sculptures and carvings. It is one of the oldest temples in Pune and is renowned for its simplicity and serene ambiance. The temple complex includes a main sanctum with a Shiva Linga and an adjoining Nandi shrine. Pataleshwar Cave Temple is an important cultural and historical site, attracting visitors interested in ancient architecture and spiritual heritage. Its location amidst a bustling city makes it a unique retreat into history.
