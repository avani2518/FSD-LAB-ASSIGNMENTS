Discover Pune's Historical Richness

Pune, a city steeped in rich heritage and history, is renowned for its fascinating historical places. Among its notable landmarks, Shaniwar Wada stands out as a majestic fortification from the Peshwa era, reflecting the grandeur of Maratha architecture. The Aga Khan Palace, with its elegant Italian arches, holds historical significance for its association with Mahatma Gandhi and the Indian independence movement. Sinhagad Fort, perched on a hilltop, offers panoramic views and a glimpse into numerous historic battles. Other significant sites include the Pataleshwar Cave Temple, an ancient rock-cut temple, and the Lal Mahal, a historical palace associated with Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj.
